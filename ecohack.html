The Dummies Guide to Climate Policy

 This project was created in Unity during the 48 hour Unity College XR Hackathon around the prompt of climate change.

 We decided to create a VR game based on passing climate policies to balance approval ratings, budget, and enviromental wellness. Players get to experience real world policy making(for example global warming and sea level rising policies) at the UN headquarters where they are given 5 proposals but need to pass three. If the players are unsure, they can consult with climate advisors who each have their own flair and advice.

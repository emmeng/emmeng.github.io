A Field of Dailies



 Dailies is a task app centered around mindfulness and growing AR Plants built in Unity.

 This project is part of ARI's(Alternate Reality Initiative, XR club at UMICH) project team that I lead this semester with a focus on teaching members the basics of XR development(coding in Unity, 3D modeling, and the LightShip SDK ). Users will be able to add daily and weekly tasks to complete, and will be rewarded with gaining items to take of a virtual AR Plant.

Cafe Bonbon was created on the Arduino while wired to a Wii nunchuck.

 For our final showcase in EECS183, we were assigned to create anything with the Arduino and we decided with a set of cute and colorful cafe-themed mini-games. The Nunchuck gave us access to its thumbstick and tilt values to make it more fun for users.

We ended up recieving 1st, the Grand Prize at the showcase.

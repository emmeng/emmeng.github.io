A2GO

 A2GO was created in Unity.

 A2GO is a solution to help newcomers to the University of Michigan explore campus and make it fun by allowing them to get tree seeds and plant them. As the university is infamous for its squirrels, there is a 10% chance that a tree will spawn a squirrel that the user has to walk over to defeat.

Visit Detroit

 This project is in collaboration with Visit Detroit, Detroit's official tourism bureau to create a city tourism AR app with ARI, the XR club at UofM.

 It is currently in the works as of Fall 2023 with the concepting phase finishing in September and the building phase to run frm October - December.

 As of now, we're currently building experimental mini-projects using Google's ARCore Geospatial API showing proof of concept for AR navigation, device localization, and AR anchors to overlay digital content on the city.

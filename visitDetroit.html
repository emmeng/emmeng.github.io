Visit Detroit

OVERVIEW

 This project represents a collaborative effort with Visit Detroit, Detroit's official tourism bureau, aimed at developing an innovative city tourism Augmented Reality (AR) application. With ARI, the XR club at the University of Michigan, we are crafting an immersive digital experience that showcases Detroit's vibrant culture and attractions for the 19 million tourists who come yearly.

DEVELOPMENT STACK

  Our technical approach involves Unity(C#) and Google's ARCore Geospatial API. Through this, we are demonstrating the feasibility of AR navigation, precise device localization, and the integration of AR anchors to seamlessly overlay digital content onto the cityscape.

MY ROLE

 In my current role for this project, I serve as the lead designer overseeing the UI/UX aspects. Simultaneously, I play a vital role in developing the code responsible for device localization, a crucial element in the presentation of AR projections on buildings.

DETAILS

 It is currently in the works as of Fall 2023 with the concepting phase finishing in September and the building phase to run from October - December.
